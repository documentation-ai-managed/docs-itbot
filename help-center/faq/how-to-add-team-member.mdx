description: Steps to invite a teammate to your ITBot workspace.
---

## Adding Team Members

1. Go to your ITBot dashboard.
2. Click on **Team** in the sidebar.
3. Select **Invite Member**.
4. Enter the teammate's email address.
5. Choose their role (Admin, Member, Viewer).
6. Click **Send Invite**.

They'll get an email with instructions to join your workspace. If not received, check spam folder or resend the invite from the dashboard.
