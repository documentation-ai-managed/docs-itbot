---
title: Creating an Auto-Response in ITBot
description: Set up ITBot to automatically reply to messages.
---

## Overview
Let ITBot reply to common questions automatically.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Automations">
    Open the ITBot dashboard and click **Automations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Rule">
    Click **New Rule** and select **Auto-Response**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Trigger & Response">
    Set the trigger phrase (e.g., `help`) and set your custom reply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Test">
    Save the rule. Send the trigger message in chat to see ITBot respond.
  </Step>
</Steps>
