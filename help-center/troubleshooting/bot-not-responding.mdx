---
title: ITBot Not Responding
description: Quick fix if ITBot isn't replying to your commands.
---

## Symptoms
- No reply from ITBot in your chat platform
- Commands appear ignored

## Possible Causes
- ITBot offline
- Integration permissions changed

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Bot Status">
    ````bash
    Visit dashboard and check if status is "Online".
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect Integration">
    Go to **Settings > Integrations** and click **Reconnect** for your chat platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>
